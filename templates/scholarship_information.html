Scholarship Information

Hamilton College is committed to providing broad access to higher education. Our scholarships are an investment in the future of our students and the future of the communities they serve as advocates for positive growth.

GWUA offers merit-based scholarships for academic and professional accomplishments as well as some need based. Many scholarships are available to our international students from all over the world.

For more information on scholarships, email us at registrar@gwua.net. For inquiries, please visit the scholarship section for the application process. For you to be considered for any scholarship, you must submit an application before any consideration.
