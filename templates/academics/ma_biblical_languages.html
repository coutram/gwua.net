Master of Arts in Biblical Languages

Overview

The Bible is the most important book ever given to mankind. And it deserves to be read with the thoughtfulness that can only come from understanding it in its original language. With the variety of study tools available today, more and more students are opting to forego language study. But that puts them forever at the mercy of the interpreters and robs them of the rich meaning and nuanced understanding that can only be appreciated by a firm grasp of the biblical languages.

The MABL degree is designed to immerse students in biblical Hebrew and Greek so they understand not only the technical grammatical aspects of the languages, but also the richness of the biblical text itself. Students will be challenged to translate large portions of Scripture in order to gain a proficiency in reading and understanding the texts in their original languages.

Intended audience

The MABL degree is designed for experienced ministers or Bible teachers who wish to increase their understanding of the original text of Scripture so they are equipped to teach it in a way that is harmonious with its original intention. It is also suitable for serious laypeople who desire to deepen their understanding of the truths of Scripture as originally taught.
