Master of Arts in Christian Education

The Masters of Arts in Christian Education consists of 36 semester hours of course work. All students in the College of Christian Education will take 30 hours of core subjects and 6 hours in a concentration to prepare them to serve in a variety of ministry roles.

Core Subjects (30 Semester Hours)

  • CE 500 Introduction to Christian Education (3)
  • CE 501 Educational Psychology (3)
  • CE 503 Philosophy and Principles of Christian Education (3)
  • CE 509 Children's Ministry in the Growing Church (3)
  • CE 511 Family Life Ministry (3)
  • CE 515 Developing a Total Church Education Program (3)
  • CE 519 Christian History and Heritage (3)
  • CE 521 Program Development, Implementation and Evaluation (3)
  • CE 530 Developing Lay Leaders and Lay Pastors in the Church (3)
  • CE 533 Church Staff and Members Relations Practicum (3)

Concentrations (6 Semester Hours)

The concentration will be a practicum in a Christian education field under the supervision of a college professor and a working Christian education professional. The following roles or fields are available for the concentration part of the Master of Arts in Christian Education:

  • Adult Ministry
  • Business Ministry
  • Children's Ministry
  • Collegiate Ministry
  • Counseling Ministry
  • General Ministry
  • Student Ministry
  • Youth Ministry
  • Worship Ministry
