The Bachelor of Science in Business Administration

The Bachelor of Science in Business Administration (BSBA) prepares graduates for a wide rang of managerial positions in business, government, and non-profit organizations. Business Administration students acquire fundamental as well as practical and professional skills in all phases of business including decision-making and problem-solving capabilities.

The BS program in Business Administration offers area concentrations that enable student to tailor their degrees to their career and education goals. Specialization is available in General Business or Leadership and Management

All students in the BS in Business Administration program must complete the courses outlined in Area One: Nine Courses (Core Components), Area Two: Five Courses (General Studies Component), and Area Three: Six Courses (Specialization in General Business) or Area Four: Six Courses (Specialization in Leadership and Management).
