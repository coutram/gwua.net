Reciprocity Certification - Certified Medical Office Manager (CMMP)

Reciprocity for the CMMP designation has been extended to candidates who hold the follow credentials:

  • CPC - American Academy of Professional Coders
  • CMM - Professional Association of Health Care office Managers
  • CPM - Professional Association of Office Mangers of America

For a limited time we are allowing holders of these designations to sit for the CMMP certification without having to complete the formal course work.

In making this offer we assume that credentialed individuals are experienced professionals and have demonstrated that they have the ability to complete both our basic and advanced medical office management training programs.

However candidates who would like to earn the CMMP designation will be provided the study materials so that they may sit for the certification exam. The CMMP Certification Examination is administered online.
