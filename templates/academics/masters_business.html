The Masters in Business Administration

The MBA program offers a broad-based education, with a choice of concentrations. The program features a curriculum that prepares adult students for the emerging knowledge economy while emphasizing the latest concepts, practices, and skills in today's rapidly changing marketplace. It is designed for working professionals from a wide range of backgrounds who wish to advance or enhance their business careers.

The program offers specializations that allow students to tailor their degrees to fit their careers and learning goals.

Undergraduates perquisites may be require for entry into the graduate program; students who have not earned degrees from appropriate fields of study may be required to take additional coursework as a perquisite for completing the program.

It is an exciting educational option for students looking to increase their understanding, advance their careers, and expand their opportunities in the following areas: Human Resource Management, Leadership and Management, Marketing, and Organizational Development and Effectiveness, General Business and Marketing and Sales.
