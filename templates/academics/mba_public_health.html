Executive Master of Business Administration in Global Public Health Management

One year program of twelve courses. The program is completed online and at one four-week sessions in North America, Canada and Bahamas (two weeks in each location). Entry to the program is based on life and work experiences.

E MBA PROGRAM COMPONENTS

The components of the EMBAB program outlined below provide formal and informal training and development experiences that require attending one four-week sessions in Canada and Bahamas, working closely with coaches via telephone or e-mail, and completing a project concerning your organization with the assistance of your manager and coach.

  • Confidential Individual Assessment

  • Development of Personal Executive Development Plan

  • Onsite Classes and Online Instruction

  • Guest Speakers at Onsite Executive Class Sessions In Canada and Bahamas

  • All examinations are available in Chinese, French or English
