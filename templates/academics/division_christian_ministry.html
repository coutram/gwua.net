Division of Christian Ministry (CCM)

The Division of Christian Ministries offers both academic and vocational degree programs designed to prepare men and women for professional service in Christian ministry and community service, including Christian education. We offer flexible and affordable distance and online learning programs from the Master of Arts in Ministry to the Doctor of Ministry degree. We have a commitment to you to deliver high-quality education to the nations of the world in the spirit of Christian evangelism and service. Among the purposes of the college is to help you become the pastor, youth pastor, evangelist, missionary, etc. that God is calling you to be. We believe that in training Christian leaders three areas are important: solid academic preparation, action application opportunities, and experienced and dedicated professors.

Three Degrees are available in the Division of Christian Ministry:
* MA in Church Leadership and Administration (coming in the fall)
  • Master of Arts in Christian Education
  • Master of Arts in Christian Ministry
