PHILOSOPHY OF THE DEPARTMENT OF NURSING

The faculty of the department of Nursing, in accordance with GWUAB, believes that every individual has biological, psychological, socio-cultural characteristics. We believe that human beings interact dynamically with their environment and this entitles them to an environment which fosters individual self-esteem and respect. We hold in high regard the inherent worth and dignity of all individuals.

Nursing

Baccalaureate nursing education is based on liberal arts and sciences. This foundation distinguishes baccalaureate nurses by preparing them to communicate, evaluate and provide humanistic health care. Professional nursing education is a combination of nursing's scientific body of knowledge and the results of nursing research.

Baccalaureate education prepares the Registered Nurse to practice in a variety of health care settings in a leadership/management role.

Mission

The mission of the Department of Nursing (BSN) is to prepare graduates to function as primary health care providers engaged in a broad range of health promotion, maintenance, and restoration to diverse populations in a variety of Health Care Environments.
