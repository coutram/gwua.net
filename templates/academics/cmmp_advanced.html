Medical Office Manager Training & Certification

The Advanced Medical Office Management Course

The advanced course is a 45 clock hour program in advanced topics of medical office management & administration. This program is designed to sharpen the skills of the medical office (practice) manager. The advanced course is designed for individuals who are currently working as a medical office (practice) manager. The program is offered via distance learning and examinations are administered online. Completion of this program also prepares you to take the CMMP certification examination.

Length of Program

The length of this program ranges from 4 weeks to 6 weeks. Program completion is dependent on the student's ability to complete the sections and tasks assigned. Once all sections are completed, the student will be required to take a timed final exam. Successful completion of this advanced course with a passing score of 70%, will entitle you to sit for the CMMP certification examination.

Grade Requirements

Must score a minimum of "70%" or better.

Admission Requirements

Prospective students for the Advanced Medical Office Manager Training program should have at least graduated from high school or earned a GED certificate. In addition candidates should have at least two years experience working in a management or supervisory position in a medical (healthcare) practice or must have completed the Basic Medical Office Management Training program.
