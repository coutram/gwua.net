College of Health Sciences

Humans throughout our history strove to overcome illnesses, injury and other health related issues. This has always been challenging, however, the advances in the sciences, especially applied sciences with its research base have contributed to the advances in the health sciences.

The aim of health sciences is to ensure that human beings enjoy optimum health with the use of applied science in health related areas.

The College of Health Sciences seeks to prepare students to participate actively in the different areas of specialization in health sciences. The areas of nursing, public and community health, and healthcare management and administration are offered at the College of Health Sciences.
