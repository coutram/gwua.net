Associated Degree in Early Childhood Education

The Associates of Science Degree in Early Childhood Education (ECE) requires 60 credit hours. Thirty credit hours in ECE education courses and 30 credit hours in general education electives.

Core Courses


Required

  • ECE 100: Introduction to ECE (3)
  • ECE 101: ECE Administration 1 (3)
  • ECE 103: ECE Administration 2 (3)
  • ECE 105: Introduction to Socialization of Children (3)
  • ECE 107: Curriculum for ECE Programs (3)
  • ECE 109: Health, Nutrition and First Aid for ECE (3)

General Specialty Electives


Select Four Courses

  • ECE 201: Supporting and Leading Teams in ECE (3)
  • ECE 202: Child, Family and Community Relations (3)
  • ECE 204: Supervision for Infant and Toddlers (3)
  • ECE 206: Children with Exceptionalities (3)
  • ECE 208: Educational Technology (3)
  • ECE 212: Language, Literacy Experiences for CEC (3)
  • ECE 301: Understanding Children Development (3)
  • ECE 302: Review of ECE and Education Principles and Theories (3)
