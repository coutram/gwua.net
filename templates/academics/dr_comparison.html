Comparison of Doctoral Degrees

Common characteristics: All four degrees are considered to be terminal and the highest degree for their particular profession and purpose. At Georgetown Wesleyan University of the Americas, all four are available in-service and share core courses in research.  For the Doctor of Ministry, the candidate may select either the dissertation (thesis), project or practicum.  For the Doctor of Philosophy, the candidate must write a dissertation. For the Doctor of Business Administration,  the candidate may do either the dissertation, the practicum or project. For the Doctor of Education, the candidate may do either the practicum or project.  Depending upon the student, circumstances, career goals, or perceived needs, the DBA, the PhD, EdD or DMin degree may be just what they are seeking.

To earn a doctoral degree the student will need to produce a dissertation, project or practicum of approximate 80,000 words under the supervision of an Advisor Professor/ Major Professor and the Doctoral Committee. As with the master's thesis, when applying for a doctoral program students should have a strong background knowledge in the subject they want to study and an idea of what they want to research. An Advisor Professor/ Major Professor and Doctoral Committee of three members will assist the student in the journey of planning, researching and writing the dissertation, project or practicum. In the two one-week meetings at a designated center, these professors will work closely with students in this process.

The dean of the college/school in which the doctoral candidate is enrolled will determine the location of the two one-week residence sessions.  During these times, the candidate, advisor professor, and the Doctoral Committee will work together on the dissertation or project.  These residence requirements are usually held the third week in November of each year.

The doctoral candidate must exhibit original research and writing by preparing a dissertation, project or practicum. A final oral examination in defense of the dissertation, practicum or project is administered after the dissertation or project is completed.  The student must complete the dissertation, project or practicum and pass the final oral examination within a maximum of five calendar years after being admitted to candidacy.  These activities will be held in the second one-week residence requirement.
