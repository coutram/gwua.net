The mission of Georgetown Wesleyan University of the Americas is to provide the educational experience in an environment supportive of faith and learning that will encourage adult students to “discover their potential.” Georgetown Wesleyan University of the Americas make high quality, professionally enhanced programs accessible to adults of diverse backgrounds and assists them to succeed in their careers and communities. Georgetown Wesleyan University of the Americas is Christian and evangelical, in nature. However, many students at Georgetown Wesleyan University of the Americas are not Christians. Students are admitted to Georgetown Wesleyan University without consideration to race, color, national origin, sex, or religion. All are encouraged to be faithful to their faith.

The core values of the University include:

Academic quality - We are committed to effective adult learning and rigorous academic standards.
Student success - We provide the opportunity and supportive learning environment that enables adults to achieve their professional and personal goals.
Educational access - We believe all desiring and qualified adults have a right to higher education.
Private and non-governmental - Georgetown Wesleyan University of the Americas is a private and non-governmental institution.
Religious emphasis - We believe that secular and religious instructions should be available to everyone in their studies to include:
  • Challenging students to respond to the spiritual and social needs of the community and world;
  • Providing a solid core of academic studies within the theological, counseling, and business disciplines;
  • Engaging students in religious experimental learning and integrative academic reflection; and
  • Promote evangelical Christianity from a Wesleyan perspective.
