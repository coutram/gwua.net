Mission Statement

The mission of Hamilton College of the Bahamas is to provide the educational experience in an environment supportive of faith and learning that will encourage adult students to "discover their potential." Hamilton College makes high quality professionally enhanced programs accessible to adults of diverse backgrounds and assists them to succeed in their careers and communities. Students are admitted to Hamilton College of the Bahamas without consideration to race, color, national origin, sex, or religion. All are encouraged to be faithful to their faith.

The core values of the University include:

  • Academic quality - We are committed to effective adult learning and rigorous academic standards.
  • Student success - We provide the opportunity and supportive learning environment that enables adults to achieve their professional and personal goals.
  • Educational access - We believe all desiring and qualified adults have a right to higher education.
  • Private and non-governmental - Hamilton College of the Bahamas is a private and non-governmental institution.

Educational Objectives

At Hamilton College of the Bahamas our goal is to provide a superior level of undergraduate and graduate education to working adult learner, who desire to advanced their career, start or grow a business or improve their job position, and prepare for religious service.

Hamilton College of the Bahamas is committed to excellent, respect for others, the distance learning model or education and making our educational offerings affordable. Our goal is to offer our educational services at an affordable price, by the use of conservative business practices, innovation, technology and forward thinking.

We realize that working professionals, due to life and career requirements, simply do not have the time to sit in a class room. So we have designed our degree programs to work around out student's schedule. As such we offer degree programs via distant learning.

Instructional Method - Distance Learning (Internet)

Hamilton College of the Bahamas of the Americas is a religious based, career focused, distance learning school. Our courses are offered via the internet, by way of our educational software portal.

Residence and Time Requirements

Since our programs are delivered via the internet, there is no classroom attendance required. However in order to successfully complete any of our degree programs, a student should be prepared to devotes at least 10-15 hrs per week in their studies. In addition most of our degree programs require missionary or ministry service.

Operating Authority

Hamilton College of the Bahamas is approved by the Bahamian Government and has been recognized by the Ministry of Education.

Online Services and Library Facilities

Hamilton College of the Bahamas delivers its programs via the internet by way of an educational software portal. We have developed a software educational delivery platform to handle our internet courses.

Our educational portal may be accessed by any standard internet browser. Once admitted to our programs, students are given a unique username and password so that they may access the educational portal and begin their course Also Hamilton College of the Bahamas does not maintain a traditional library. We do however maintain an electronic library that is accessible by all of our students.

Statement of Legal Control

Hamilton College of the Bahamas of the Americas Bahamas is a registered corporation in the Bahamas. We are governed by an advisory board and headed by a President.

The President of Hamilton College of the Bahamas is Dr. Jothi Jothishankar.
